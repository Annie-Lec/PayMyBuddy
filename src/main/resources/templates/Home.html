Welcome to "Pay My Buddy" !

With Pay My Buddy, you can transfer or receive money to your friends easily and with immediate effect.

You just have to :
- 1 - create a Profile (including bank details to load your Buddy balance before performing any transaction)
- 2 - fill in your contacts who must also be part of "Pay my Buddy"
- 3 - go to the Transfer menu to make a transfer to your buddy instantly.
